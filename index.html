FaQ

Can't find the answer you're lookig for? We've shared some of our most frequently asked questions to help you out!

Covid FaQ's:

Will I receive a refund in full, if the event will be cancelled due to COVID-19?

We are living in very uncertain times so, we are committing to refund all the costs

How are you going to minimise the chance of transmission?

It is mandatory to take a covid 19 test before attending the event

Will there be a testing area at the event?

There will be a testing facility before the entrance to the event

Will maintaining social distancing and wearing a mask be mandatory at the event?

Of course, these rules will be followed and monitored by festival staff

Do I have to bring evidence in the form of a negative antigen test result to attend the event?

You can attach the test result when buying your ticket online

Event FaQ's:

Can I bring my own alcohol/food?

It is forbidden to bring alcohol to the festival from outside although you are allowed to bring your own food and soft drinks like water or soda

Where can I buy tickets for the festival?

You can buy them at the ticket station at the event or through our ticket provider ticketmaster

Is there a parking spot at the festival?

We do provide a parking spot but with limited parking slots

Can I bring my own tent?

Yes but it has to be in plain color with no logo. Also it and its dimensions cannot be greater than 210 x 240 x 170 cm

Is there overnight security? if so what kind of security do you provide?

Yes, overnight security monitors are displayed all over the facility. Level-UP festival is not responsible for stolen items
